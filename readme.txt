-------------------------------------------------------
    LSX Features:
-------------------------------------------------------

- Theme Options:
Basic tab covers important basics like logo, favicon and a few others.

- Widgets:
LSX offers 1 blog flexible widget

- Page Templates:
There is plenty of that. 9 Templates are very much self-explanatory
once you're on done with the content on your page, go to Page Attributes
on the right and choose any available Template.

-Sidebars:
Sidebar, Sidebar - Secondary and Footer. 

- Menus
There is one primary menu.
